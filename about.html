About our company

A total logistics service provider, born in Cebu.

Lcargo Diversified Logistics Inc. is a total logistics service provider located in Bridges Town Square, Plaridel St., Alang-alang, Mandaue City, Cebu. We cater to freight forwarding — international and domestic — trucking, warehousing, special cargo handling, and customs brokerage services.

The company was incorporated on September 22, 2016 with just one van. Since then we've grown our fleet to 4 prime movers, 3 wing vans, 2 closed vans, an L300, and a multicab — backed by a team of 22 — to cater to all your logistical needs. We started with 5 clients and have grown to more than 25 customers and counting.
